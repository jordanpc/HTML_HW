MVP Height Analysis

The scatter plot above depicts MVP winner height by position over the seasons.

  • 19 of the first 28 MVP winners were 6’10” (82”) or taller
  • 25 of the first 28 players played the Center or Forward-Center position
  • 12 of the last 13 most recent MVP winners were 6'9" (81") or shorter
  • Of the 12 most recent MVP winners to be 6'9" or shorter:
  • 8 played the Guard position (PG, SG, G)
  • 5 played either Small Forward or Forward
  • 1 played Power Forward
